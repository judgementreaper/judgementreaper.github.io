The Devil's Legacy

All members are expected to read and understand rules.

  • Rule #1: No Nudity, Gore, Racist, Sexist, Violent, Disturbing, Inappropriate, or Offensive text, voice, images, or videos anywhere in our Community.

    Rule #1.1: Attempting to circumvent these rules through direct message, custom status, profile picture, and/or nickname is not allowed.

  • Rule #2: Be respectful to all members of the community.

  • Rule #3: Please keep conflict out of the community, this extends to DMs.

  • Rule #4: Follow instructions from all our staff. Any issues with a Officer or Moderator, can be directed to an Admin.

  • Rule #5: Advertisement of another Discord, Social Media, or Community Websites are not allowed and will result in a ban.

  • Rule #6: Never impersonate staff or another member of the Community.

  • Rule #7: Keep all political discussion out of the community.
