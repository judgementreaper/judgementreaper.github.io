The Devil's Legacy


Welcome

Good news guardians, The Devil’s Legacy has become large enough to split into two clans! We will be using discord to coordinate between the clans and will likely be expanding again soon. This means it’s more important than ever to have everyone active in the discord server. To make things easier, and help everyone easily join groups with other clan members, we have decided to have each clan represent a regional time zone. This will also give members more opportunities to fill leadership positions in each clan. If you are not in the correct clan, you can apply by clicking on the appropriate link in the Join TDL section of this page.


Statement

TDL was created during the Destiny One days. We are a chill clan that is for mature players only. We want everyone to feel welcome, and will not tolerate players that break our Code of Conduct.



Join TDL

To simplify the process of finding fireteams, we have decided to separate the clans by region. By doing this, guardians will be more likely to see online players, creating more opportunities to complete activities & clan bounties.
